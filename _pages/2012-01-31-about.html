Hey, I'm Michael. Since March 2013, I work at Spotify's Stockholm office as a Software Developer.

Before I joined Spotify, I spent a year and a half working as a Developer at WDS in Poole, a town in southern England. I arrived in England after having lived in smoggy but always exciting Shanghai for a year, where I was working as an Intern Developer at Ebay and studying as an exchange student at Tongji University. The exchange was made possible through Uppsala University, where I studied Computer Science. If you've got the opportunity to move abroad for a while, just do it, it's given me so many valuable experiences.

I hope to use this space to write about my thoughts on things related to software and the tech-side of the on-demand music-streaming industry. I like open source and I try to be active through Github. If you want to contact me, please leave a comment or throw me an email at thelinmichael at Google's mail service.
